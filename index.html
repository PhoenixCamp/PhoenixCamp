Phoenix Camp NYC

July 15-17, 2016

@ United Nations

Phoenix Camp NYC is a community-run conference focused on the open source Phoenix Framework

The conference will involve a mix of trainings (Fri), sessions/keynotes (Sat/Sun), and contribution sprints (Sun)

Propose Your Session (Until Jul 6)

Keynote by Dave Thomas

Sat Jul 16 from 1-2 PM

We're very fortunate to have Dave Thomas joining to deliver a keynote.

Dave is a computer programmer, author & editor. He co-authored The Pragmatic Programmer and co-created The Pragmatic Bookshelf publishing company, which he until May 2015

Dave coined the phrases 'Code Kata' and 'DRY' (Don't Repeat Yourself), and was an original signatory and author of The Manifesto for Agile Software Development.
